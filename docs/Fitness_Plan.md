# Tidal Power Fitness: Beginner Strength & Conditioning Plan

**Focus:** Leg Strength & Core Stability (Push Emphasis)
**Goal:** Build foundational lower body strength and core endurance.

## Workout Routine

| Exercise | Sets | Reps | Notes |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Squats** | 3 | 10 | Keep chest up, hips back, and knees tracking over toes. |
| **Lunges** | 3 | 10 | 10 reps per leg. Keep torso upright. |
| **Farmers Carry** | 3 | 10 | Walk for approx. 30-60s. Maintain a strong, upright posture. |

### Core Circuit
*Perform 3 rounds with minimal rest between exercises.*

1.  **Dead Bug**: 30 seconds active / 30 seconds rest. Focus on keeping your lower back pressed into the floor.
2.  **Plank**: 1 minute active / 30 seconds rest. Maintain a straight line from head to heels.

## Warm-up & Cool-down

*   **Warm-up:** 5 minutes.
    *   Light cardio (brisk walk, elliptical).
    *   Dynamic stretching (leg swings, hip circles).
*   **Cool-down:** 5 minutes.
    *   Static stretching (quad stretch, hamstring stretch).
    *   Foam rolling (optional).

## Rest Days
*   **Recovery:** Take at least one rest day between heavy leg sessions to allow for muscle repair and growth.
*   **Active Recovery:** Light activities such as walking, swimming, or yoga are encouraged on rest days to promote blood flow.

## Nutrition Guidelines
*   **Hydration:** Drink plenty of water before, during, and after your workout to maintain performance.
*   **Protein:** Consume a protein-rich meal or snack within 2 hours of your workout to support muscle repair.
*   **Balance:** Aim for a balanced diet of lean proteins, complex carbohydrates, and healthy fats.
